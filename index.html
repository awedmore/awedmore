In-Branch Teller Interactions

Transaction Processing

Replaced 7 SOAP APIs used for transaction processing with 4 modernized RESTful APIs. Coordinated 3 deveopment teams and Frontline Operations, Risk, Finance, and Settlement teams to build, test, and release the changes in 5 months. The new APIs improved transaction processing time by 25%.

Mobile-First Cash Withdrawals

Managed the development of both iOS and Android versions of a new mobile centric cash withdrawal experience. Created the pilot release plan to a small test population, coordinated closely with enterprise level platform teams to integrate feature into the main codebase, and performed rigorous testing to ensure desired performance.

ATM Data Analysis and Reporting

Modernized and enhanced internal data collection from and reporting on ATM fleet health. Implemented daily reports sent via email and Slack to Operations and Frontline stakeholders, alerting of current or potential issues with ATM hardware. The new reporting reduced time to detect issues by 2 days. The enhanced data collection removed dependencies on external teams to collect and send ATM logs, greatly decreasing the time and effort needed to troubleshoot both hardware and software issues in the fleet.
